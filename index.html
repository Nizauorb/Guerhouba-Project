A lot is planned and we hope this little version will be fun to play!/!\ You take on the role of an alchemist, a researcher lost in a desert who is beginning to learn how to use his power to fight against the corruption of the desert. Survive over 3 days, protect the train altar and escape the introductory area. Features :
  • Avatar can slide and glide and dash
  • 5 differents bad guys to fight
  • 9 spells to upgrade and use
  • 1 event to do and repeat
